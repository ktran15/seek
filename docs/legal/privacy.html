Seek — Privacy Policy

Effective July 7, 2026 · Contact: alvaradof010407@gmail.com

Seek is a social challenge app: you complete a daily challenge, submit photo or video proof, and share it with friends. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. The short version: we collect only what the app needs to work, we don't sell it, and we don't run ads or trackers.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address (or the identity your Apple/Google sign-in shares), a username, an optional display name, and your avatar choices.
  • Submitted media — the photos, videos, and screenshots you submit as challenge proof, and images you attach to comments. Proof is the product: what you submit is visible to other users per the app's visibility rules (friends, friends-of-friends, and the public Explore feed).
  • Activity — challenge submissions and scores, friendships and invites, head-to-head results, votes, likes, comments, reports and blocks, and your in-app coins, points, crates, and cosmetics.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising and no third-party ad trackers.
  • No sale or rental of your data to anyone.
  • No cross-app tracking — the app never asks for iOS App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not track you.

How your data is used

Only to run the app: showing your posts to the right audiences, pairing head-to-head matches, tallying votes, ranking the friends leaderboard, paying in-app rewards, sending the notifications you enable, and keeping the community safe (reports, blocks, and moderation).

Where it lives

Data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase, in access-controlled databases and private media storage. Media is served through short-lived signed URLs, not public links.

Moderation

Content reported by users is reviewed and may be removed. Removed content disappears from all feeds but may be retained briefly for safety review.

Deleting your account

Settings → Delete account permanently deletes your profile, submissions and media, social connections, and in-app economy data. This is immediate and cannot be undone. Records of matches you played against other users remain in their history with your identity removed.

Children

Seek is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and note the new effective date above. Material changes will be called out in the app.
